Phuket Paradise:
13-Day Golf & Luxury Getaway

7 rounds of golf on Asia's premier courses · 12 nights 4-star Patong Beach · Personal caddies every round · All transfers included

About This Tour

One of our absolute favourites, and for good reason. 13 days and 12 nights of paradise, including 7 rounds of golf on some of Asia's finest courses. You'll be based at the wonderful 4-star The Kee Resort & Spa, right in the heart of the action at Patong Beach, steps from great restaurants, the beach, and the vibrant energy of Phuket's most exciting strip.

Imagine spending your mornings on lush, beautifully designed fairways, your afternoons poolside with a cold Singha, and your evenings experiencing the vibrant atmosphere of Bangla Road and Patong Beach. This is not just a golf trip, it's a complete experience.

The Golf

Seven rounds across Phuket's finest courses, each one handpicked by Dean for exceptional conditions, genuine challenge, and outstanding scenery:

  • Red Mountain Golf Club
    Built around an extinct volcanic quarry, dramatic red rock formations, spectacular elevation changes, and arguably the most visually stunning course in Phuket. A must-play.
  • Blue Canyon Country Club
    Host to the Johnnie Walker Classic and home to some of the most memorable holes in Asia. Two courses, the Canyon Course and the Lakes Course, both exceptional.
  • Laguna Phuket Golf Club
    Set within the stunning Laguna Phuket resort complex, winding through tropical landscaped gardens, lakes, and lagoons. Superb conditions and facilities.
  • Phuket Country Club
    A classic Phuket course with tropical tree-lined fairways, excellent greens, and the wonderful service that defines Thai golf. Our caddies here are outstanding.
  • Aquella Golf & Country Club + one more
    Plus a spectacular round in Phang Nga Province, our personal favourite addition to any Phuket tour, offering stunning views and a completely different landscape.

Accommodation

Your base for the duration is The Kee Resort & Spa, Patong Beach, a stylish 4-star property perfectly positioned at the heart of Phuket's most vibrant area. The resort features beautiful pool facilities, an excellent restaurant, and rooms designed for relaxed tropical living. After a day on the course, you'll love coming back here.
