Our Story

Built on a Mate's Dream. Carried Forward with Pride.

Prestige Golf Tours was founded by Michael Patterson, a passionate golfer and natural connector who believed that the best golf trips aren't just about the courses, they're about the people you share them with. For a decade, Patto built something special: a community of golfers who kept coming back, and a reputation built entirely on word of mouth.

When Michael passed away, his mate Dean Page stepped up to carry the torch, and honour everything Patto had built. Over the past five years, Dean has taken the business to new heights: opening up Vietnam and the Philippines, forging direct relationships with Asia's finest courses, and expanding the kinds of experiences on offer while keeping the same easy-going, no-fuss spirit that made Prestige Golf what it is.

Today, Prestige Golf Tours is run in Patto's memory, with the same soul he put into it, and a lot more destinations to explore. Every tour is a tribute to what he started.

As Mick Munane (PGA Professional) puts it: "Dean is a Proper Golf Tragic, passionate about the game, loves playing his best, and enjoys the social side too. He has done all the hard work for you, found the best places to stay, the bars with the coldest beer, and you are free to enjoy yourself."

What Makes Us Different

Not Just a Tour Operator. A Fellow Golfer.

Everything we do is designed by golfers, for golfers, with the standards we'd demand for ourselves.

Every Course & Hotel Hand-Selected

We don't add courses or hotels based on brochure photos. Every property on our itineraries has been personally vetted, chosen for quality, conditions, and the kind of experience our golfers deserve.

Direct Relationships on the Ground

Our years of touring mean we have genuine relationships with course managers, hotel staff, and local operators, giving our groups priority tee times, better access, and real flexibility.

Small Groups, Big Experience

We deliberately keep group sizes small. This means more flexibility, more personal service, and the kind of camaraderie that turns a good golf trip into a legendary one.

PGA-Endorsed & Trusted

We're endorsed by PGA Professionals who have personally experienced our tours. Their trust reflects the standard we hold ourselves to, one that serious golfers can rely on.

Transparent, All-Inclusive Pricing

Every price we quote is complete. Green fees, caddies, carts, accommodation, breakfast, transfers, taxes, all included. No surprises when you arrive. No awkward extras.

Australian-Owned, No Language Barriers

You're dealing with a fellow Australian throughout, before, during, and after your trip. No middlemen, no lost-in-translation moments, no offshore call centres.
