About This Tour

Embark on an 8-day golf adventure that captures the essence of Vietnam's premier courses. Da Nang, "Asia's most liveable city", sits on a spectacular stretch of coastline surrounded by some of the continent's most exciting new golf developments.

This tour pairs world-class golf with the rich cultural experience of Central Vietnam, ancient Hội An, the UNESCO World Heritage site of Mỹ Sơn, the spectacular Ba Na Hills with its famous Golden Bridge, and the fresh seafood and flavours of a Vietnamese coastal city that hasn't been overrun by mass tourism.

Da Nang is rising fast on the global golf radar, and rightly so. The quality of courses here rivals anything in Thailand, at often more favourable prices. If you haven't played Vietnam yet, this is the trip to do it.

The Golf Courses

  • Hoiana Shores Golf Club
    Designed by Robert Trent Jones Jr., this spectacular links-style course hugs the South China Sea coastline. Ocean breezes, dramatic coastal scenery, and golf that will challenge and delight at every level. Consistently ranked among Vietnam's best.
  • Montgomerie Links Vietnam
    Colin Montgomerie's signature design, a stunning championship course with beautifully contoured fairways, strategic water features, and spectacular views of the Trường Sơn Mountains. One of the finest courses in Indochina.
  • Laguna Golf Lăng Cô
    A Sir Nick Faldo design nestled between the mountains and Lang Co Bay, one of the most strikingly beautiful settings in Asian golf. World-class facilities within the stunning Laguna Lăng Cô resort complex.
  • BRG Da Nang Golf Resort
    Designed by Pete Dye along the South China Sea beachfront, dramatic oceanside holes, superb conditions, and the sheer spectacle of playing alongside one of Asia's most beautiful beaches.

Ba Na Hills, An Unmissable Excursion

No visit to Da Nang is complete without a trip to the spectacular Ba Na Hills mountain resort. Home to the iconic Golden Bridge, held aloft by two giant stone hands and offering extraordinary views of the Vietnamese highlands, Ba Na Hills is unlike anything you'll see anywhere else in the world.

We've incorporated a Ba Na Hills excursion into the itinerary, both for the daytime spectacle and, for those who wish, the breathtaking view at night, when the mountain resort transforms into a magical illuminated world.
