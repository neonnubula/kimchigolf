Bangkok Birdies:
World-Class Golf in the City

3 exceptional rounds at Bangkok's finest courses · 6 nights 4-star · Personal caddies every round · Culture, dining and vibrant city life

About This Tour

Join us for an exciting week in Bangkok, the city with a rich tapestry of cultural heritage, modern amenities, and vibrant street life that truly offers a unique and memorable experience for all who visit.

Bangkok boasts some of Asia's finest golf courses, world-class designs, immaculate conditions, and the warmth of Thai hospitality that makes every round special. Our Bangkok Birdies tour combines three exceptional rounds with the very best the city has to offer: magnificent temples, incredible street food, rooftop bars, and the legendary energy of Bangkok nights.

This is also an ideal entry point for golfers joining us for a longer Thailand tour, the Bangkok Birdies can be combined with our Pattaya or Phuket tours for a comprehensive Thailand golf adventure.

The Golf Courses

  • Nikanti Golf Club
    Consistently ranked among Bangkok's top courses. Stunning modern design, perfectly manicured fairways framed by sugar palm trees, and service that sets the standard for Thai golf hospitality. Exceptional on every level.
  • Royal Bang Pa In Golf Club
    Set within the grounds of the historic Royal Bang Pa In Palace, one of Thailand's most treasured sites. The golf is exceptional, the setting incomparable, and the history absolutely fascinating.
  • Alpine Sports Club Bangkok
    A challenging and beautifully maintained course favoured by Bangkok's golf community. Strategic holes, excellent greens, and a wonderful atmosphere, the perfect round to round out a Bangkok week.

Bangkok Beyond Golf

On your non-golf days, Bangkok offers endless possibilities. Visit the Grand Palace and Wat Phra Kaew, take a long-tail boat through the khlongs, explore the weekend markets at Chatuchak, dine at Gaggan or Nahm (Asia's Best Restaurants), or simply wander the streets of one of the world's most fascinating cities.

Bangkok's restaurant scene is extraordinary, from $3 pad thai at street stalls to Michelin-starred fine dining. Our team will point you to the best of it.
