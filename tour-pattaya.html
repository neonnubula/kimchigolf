Party in Pattaya:
Outstanding Golf, Fascinating Nightlife

6 rounds including Siam Country Club Old Course (home of the LPGA Honda Classic) · Bangkok city nights · Optional Bangkok Extension with 3 more rounds

About This Tour

"Outstanding Golf, Fascinating Nightlife, Exquisite Scenery… Our Party in Pattaya has it all!"

Staying in 4-star accommodation right in the heart of the action, you'll be blown away with what both Pattaya and Bangkok have to offer. With an optional Bangkok Extension tour available, this is an event you'll never forget.

Pattaya has evolved into one of Asia's premier golf destinations, with courses that rival anything in the world. Yet despite the world-class golf, you're also in one of Asia's most entertaining cities. The combination is irresistible for golfers who want it all.

The Golf Courses

  • Siam Country Club, Old Course
    Home of the LPGA Honda Classic and one of Asia's most celebrated courses. Immaculate conditions, spectacular design, and a round you'll be talking about for years.
  • Siam Country Club, Rolling Hills Course
    The sister course to the Old Course, a stunning undulating layout through tropical forest with dramatic elevation changes and exceptional bunkering.
  • Siam Country Club, Plantation Course
    The third jewel in Siam CC's crown, wide fairways, strategic bunkering, and the same exceptional service and conditions that define the Siam brand.
  • Laem Chabang International Golf Club
    A Jack Nicklaus Signature Design with stunning views of the Gulf of Thailand. Set amongst tropical hills, it's one of Thailand's most admired and strategically challenging courses.
  • Ballyshear Golf Links + Royal Gems Golf City (Bangkok)
    Your Bangkok rounds include the spectacular Royal Gems Golf City, featuring stunning Augusta 15th and TPC Sawgrass 17th replicas, plus one more outstanding course.

The Bangkok Extension Option

Can't get enough? Add our Bangkok Extension and stay an additional 6 nights in Bangkok, playing 3 more rounds at world-class Bangkok courses. The extension transforms a great trip into an epic one, 9 rounds total, two incredible Thai cities, and memories to last a lifetime.
